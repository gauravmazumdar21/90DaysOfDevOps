# #90DaysOfDevOps Challenge

## Learn, Upskill, Grow with the Community

Join our DevOps community challenge starting on January 1st, 2024 and embark on a 90-day journey to become a better DevOps practitioner. This repository serves as an open invitation to all DevOps enthusiasts who are looking to enhance their skills and knowledge. By participating in this challenge, you will have the opportunity to learn from others in the community, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and ultimately strengthen your DevOps abilities.

Let's come together to grow and achieve new heights in DevOps!

## Steps:

- Fork[https://github.com/LondheShubham153/90DaysOfDevOps/fork] the Repo.
- Learn every day and add your learnings in the day-wise folders.
- Check out what others are Learning and help/learn from them.
- Showcase your learnings on LinkedIn
